nachos

Description

Nachos is a Mexican culinary dish consisting of tortilla chips or totopos covered with cheese or cheese sauce, as well as a variety of other toppings and garnishes, often including meats (such as ground beef or grilled chicken), vegetables (such as chili peppers, lettuce, tomatoes, and olives), and condiments such as salsa, guacamole, or sour cream.

Ingredients

  • tortilla chips
  • cheese sauce
  • meats

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. Place a mound of chips on a baking sheet.
  3. Add the cheese.
  4. Cook the nachos.
  5. Dress the nachos.
